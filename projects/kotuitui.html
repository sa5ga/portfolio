Kōtuitui

The challenge

Young Aucklanders feel unsafe on public transport, seeing it as more dangerous than it is. Isolated incidents and lack of visible safety measures reinforce fear, negative views and discourages use.

The outcome

A campaign that makes public transport feel safer by making drivers more visible, approachable and connected to its riders
